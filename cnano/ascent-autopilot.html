The selected ascent path.

0 = krpc_MechJeb_AscentClassic_t (Classic Ascent Profile)

1 = krpc_MechJeb_AscentGT_t (Stock-style GravityTurn)

2 = krpc_MechJeb_AscentPEG_t (Powered Explicit Guidance (RSS/RO))
